Who We Are:

  • We are a team of student volunterring and working together to help our fellow students.
  • We offer them tools and services to help learn and improve on their own.
  • This website is the main hub for all the services that Team Rakz provides.

What We Do

We provide our fellow students with the following services to help them succeed:

Lectures

We offer lectures written for various subjects in full format, written clearly and ready every weekend.

Exercises

We offer various exercises and tests. Such as official exercises provided by university faculty or useful exercies and tests provided by our members.

Reference Books

We offer links to reference books and source materials for various subjects, as well as other additional sources of information on topics

Video Lectures

We offer videos that help explain complex subjects, or even some videos of lectures at the university

Intended Users

We provide most of our serivces to engineering students at KFS University:

Prepartory

We offer our services for the prepartory year students at KFS University.

Electrical

We offer our services for the electrical department students at KFS University.

Civil

We offer our services for the civil department students at KFS University.

Mechanical

We offer our services for the mechanical department students at KFS University.

Disclaimers: Keep in mind not all these services are available for every subject we cover.

Any recording of university lectures are recorded with consent from its lecturer.
