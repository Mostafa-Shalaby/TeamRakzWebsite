About Us

The following are some of the tools we provide to help you succeed

Lectures

We offer lectures written for various subjects in full format, written clearly and ready every weekend.

Exercises

We offer various exercises and tests. Such as official exercises provided by university faculty or useful exercies and tests provided by our members.

Reference Books

We offer links to reference books and source materials for various subjects, as well as other additional sources of information on topics

Video Lectures

We offer videos that help explain complex subjects, or even some videos of lectures at the university
